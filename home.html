Welcome to Jou Jou's Bites

Welcome to Jou Jou's Bites, where we are both proud and excited to introduce you to the vibrant flavors of Taiwanese cuisine on the bustling street food scene. At Jou Jou's Bites, we offer a delightful array of options to tantalize your taste buds. Indulge in the comforting nostalgia of granny's traditional slow-cooked pork belly, evoking memories of home-cooked meals shared with loved ones. Or savor the authentic taste of Taiwanese milk tea, crafted with care and tradition.

For those with a sweet tooth, our Portuguese-inspired homemade pastel de natas promises a delectable treat that will leave you craving for more.

To embark on a culinary journey through Taiwanese cuisine, visit our food stall located at various street food markets across London each week. Additionally, we take pride in offering catering services for corporate events and special occasions. For more information or to inquire about our catering services, please click the link below to get in touch with us. We look forward to serving you soon!

Our Beginnings

As a Taiwanese native, my wife has always encouraged me to indulge in the flavors of her childhood and the cherished recipes passed down from her grandmother. Each dish she prepares is a delightful symphony of taste and tradition, leaving me in awe every time. "You could sell this," I'd often remark, impressed by her culinary prowess. So, in November 2022, we took a leap of faith and launched our first market stall. Though the hours are long, the journey has been incredibly rewarding. We've had the pleasure of meeting countless wonderful individuals and the heartwarming feedback we've received has fueled our passion even more.
